Honey Barbecue Chicken Recipe

Published: Thursday, December 12, 2019 Modified: Thursday, December 12, 2019

Pure Honey, Herbs, Spices, Wine, and Ketchup base combined to make Quick Honey Barbeque Sauce Recipes for Chicken on the Barbecue Grill, or it’s Easy inside in the oven. Moist and Tender. Let’s Barbecue!

Honey Barbecue Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 c ketchup
  • 1/2 c honey
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp season salt
  • 1/2 onion small diced
  • 1 tbsp garlic, fresh chopped
  • 1/4 c brown sugar
  • 1/8 c vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 tsp ground rosemary
  • 1/2 c red wine
  • 1/8 c molasses

Honey Barbecue Sauce Directions

Combine ingredients in sauce pan, simmer for 1/2 hour, stirring frequently. Be careful as sauce wants to splatter. Cover between stirs. Great for outdoor Summer Barbecues. Serve with salad, fruits, or side dishes

Chicken Instructions

To Cook Inside

In large roasting pan, place chicken and Roast uncovered 375℉ for 40-60 minutes. When chicken is most of the way cooked, pour Honey Barbecue Sauce over Chicken and place under broiler to finish.

To Barbecue Outside

Place chicken in pot and simmer until chicken is cooked, but not overcooked. Remove chicken and place on preheated barbecue grill. Baste with sauce several times while turning over, Grilling both sides until desired doneness is achieved. Serve with salads, rice or fruit
