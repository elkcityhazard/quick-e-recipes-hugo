## No Bake Cookies Recipe

  * 1/2 c margerine
  * 1/2 c milk
  * 1/2 c cocoa
  * 2 c sugar
  * 1/2 tsp salt

### No Bake Cookies Directions

place these ingredients in sauce pan and heat to a slow boil. Remove from heat and add:

  * 3 c oats
  * 1 tsp vanilla ext.
  * 1/2 c peanut butter

mix together, place desired size balls on wax paper to cool.
