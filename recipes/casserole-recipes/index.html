Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin Recipe

Monday, December 16, 2019

Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin Recipe Quick Easy Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin Recipe is a nice hearty hot vegetable with cream sauce and cheese topped with bread crumbs. Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin will dress up any meal for a Holidayor Special Function. Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin Ingredients Serves 8-12 1 lb broccoli 1 lb cauliflower 1/2 lb shredded cheddar 1 c bread or cracker crumbs 1/4 stick butter(melted) roux Cauliflower Broccoli Au Gratin Directions Cut broccoli and cauliflower in bite size pieces and boil for 2-4 minutes.
