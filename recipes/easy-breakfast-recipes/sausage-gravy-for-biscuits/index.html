Sausage Gravy For Biscuits

Published: Tuesday, December 10, 2019 Modified: Tuesday, December 10, 2019

Delicious homemade sausage gravy for biscuits is simple and takes about 15 minutes to prepare. Also click the link to our homemade biscuits.

Sausage Gravy For Biscuits Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground pork sausage
  • 1 tsp. rubbed sage
  • 1/4 c. diced onions
  • 1/2 tsp. chicken base
  • 1/2 tsp Chef Frank’s Seasoning
  • 1/2 c. flour
  • 2 c. milk

Sausage Gravy For Biscuits Directions

Place sausage in large frying with onions and sage. Cook thoroughly on medium heat until lightly browned. Add sage, chicken base and mix together. Gradually add flour and mix around until pasty while watching the heat. (you may not need all the flour). Add milk and stir while cooking.until milk thickens, and then it’s done. Serve over biscuits.
