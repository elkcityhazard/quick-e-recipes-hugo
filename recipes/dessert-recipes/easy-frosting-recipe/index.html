Easy Frosting Recipe

Published: Monday, December 16, 2019 Modified: Monday, December 16, 2019

Mom’s Quick Easy Frosting Recipe Makes any Cake Better. Mom’s Frosting is from Scratch and Easy to Make, and Top any Cake, Cupcakes and Birthday Cakes.

Here is a simple recipe that will wow everyone!

Easy Frosting Ingredients

  • 1/2 c milk
  • 2 1/2 tsp flour

Step 1 Directions

cook in sauce pan to make thick paste, set in refrigerator to cool

  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1/2 c butter or margerine
  • dash salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla ext.

Step 2 Directions

Blend sugar, butter and salt and add to cooled paste. Add vanilla, whip until fluffy.
